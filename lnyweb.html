Lunar New Year Gala Website

2023 LNY Gallery


Introduction

CAAL's Lunar New Year Gala has been a 40-year tradition in Lexington, and was finally back in person for the first time after the pandemic. Before the gala, the website was used to sell tickets and contained information about the program. In order to commemorate the event, we are going to be uploading all of the photos that were taken by others during the event, including photos of the volunteers, performers, and youth teams.



Project Timeline

- Week 1: Teach JS and concepts required for image gallery
- Week 2: Guide team through important styling (CSS) fixes for current website
- Week 3: Build 3 pages for event MCs, volunteers, and performers
- Week 4: Wrap up project
