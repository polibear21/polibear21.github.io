Lexington Youth STEAM Team Logo Voting

Accepting votes until 9/17


Introduction

The Lexington STEAM Youth Team was founded in 2021, and currently consists of more than 20 STEAM passionate highschoolers. the team is led by Wei Ding, a Computer Science Professor at UMass, and is a non-profit team which is part of the CAAL. The team dedicates themselves to improving the community using their computer science abilities, and has worked on projects such as the Lexington Miles & Smiles Patriot's Day event, nobel prize winner Dr. Ting's talk, the Harrington PTA website, and more.


As the STEAM team continues to work earnestly on new projects, the team decided that a logo would be important for the team's future developments. Consequently, the team extended an opportunity for submissions, and recieved various submissions.

Vote!

Now, the STEAM Team would like to ask for the community's help in deciding which logo best suits the team. Attached on this webpage is a Google Form featuring all of the submissions. Anyone can cast a vote and have their opinions factored into the selection process. After the public votes, our logo committee will hold a discussion supported by the public's opinion, and ultimately decide on a logo. We hope that you will help our team decide, thank you!
