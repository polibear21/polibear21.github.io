Who we are

Who we are

We are a group of students passionate about computer science who want to contribute to our community through community service based computer science projects. In this team we emphasize collaboration, perseverance, dedication and kindness. We work efficiently together, gain the most from every learning experience and make a difference in our communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to strengthen the bonds of the community through the development of innovative computer science projects.
