Lexington Lyceum Project


Introduction

The Lexington Lyceum Project aims to re-open the Ellen Stone Building, originally built as a lyceum. The original purpose of this building is to provide adult education and help the youth engage with civil responsibilities. The Lyceum Committee hopes the building to become an inclusive space where people express different topics and learn from others about the history of this region. In the current phase, the STEAM Team is helping with the website design of Frederick Douglass Day. In the future, the STEAM Team will continue to collaborate with the Lyceum Committee with design of event webpages and more.
